What is Technically a Coffee?
Great question.

A community meetup, started in March 2013, where every month 4 local startups demo their products to 40-80 people in the Greenville Startup Community who give feedback, beta test the products or signup as customers. It starts at 8am, is done by 9am and coffee+bagels are free. It's free to attend. Whether you are new to the Greenville Startup Community or have been here for years - you are welcome.

Do you want to present?
Let us know!

We are always looking for great companies to present at the next Technically a Coffee meetup. If you are interested in presenting or if you know a company that you'd like to see present - drop us a line below. Sometimes it may be a couple months to get on the calendar, so the earlier you reachout, the better.

Why show up?
Besides the free food and coffee?

This monthly event is run by local founders and the food+bev is provided by local startups who realize the best way to give back is to be involved. If you are looking to meet other entrepreneurs, people in tech or just learn what cool products are being built in the Greenville area, then RSVP for the next event. It's a casual group and we'd love to meetcha :)
